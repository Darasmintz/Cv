About Me

I am Olorode Oluwafunmilayo, a technology-driven youth leader with strong interests in AI prompt engineering, web development, cybersecurity awareness, and community mentorship. My journey in technology began with a passion for creating solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

Throughout my career, I have led and contributed to multiple innovation-focused initiatives aimed at education, accessibility, financial inclusion, and digital empowerment. These projects have allowed me to combine technical skills with a deep commitment to social impact.

As a Youth Tech Innovator, I focus on bridging the gap between technology and everyday challenges, ensuring that digital solutions are accessible, inclusive, and effective for diverse communities.

My Philosophy

I believe in using technology as a tool for social impact, inclusion, and sustainable development. Technology should empower, not exclude; it should solve real problems and create opportunities for all, especially the youth.
